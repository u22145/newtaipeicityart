03WHEN I’M QUIET, I LISTEN.
EAST FIELD
ALL DAYS 10:00-20:00

Amidst the cacophony of voices, the knife-like "words" shatter lives, and fragments of memories drift away with the wind, never finding a tranquil place to settle. The performance guides people to move and listen, collecting elusive sound clues that are colorless, tasteless, and indefinable. It attempts to piece together a puzzle of diverse cultures, histories, and environments, leaving behind some evidence related to existence.
