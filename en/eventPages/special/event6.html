06IT’S GAME TIME
MEET AT THE PAVILION
7/15(六) 14:00-14:30、16:00-16:30
7/16(日) 14:00-14:30、16:00-16:30

Dr. Rio, the scientist, arrives at the "New Art Land" laboratory to experiment with his new scientific ideas. Whenever he faces research obstacles or mental fatigue, he always manages to regain inspiration and scientific enthusiasm through "games"! The performance starts with interactive street art, recreating classic 80s video games like Super Mario in the real world. It leads the audience to freely roam in an imaginative and playful world!

HSU KAI-HSUAN

Kai Hsuan Hsu, graduated from the Graduate Institute of Atmospheric Physics of National Central University, is currently carrying out research on air pollution in Academia Sinica. When not at work, he performs as a street artist all around the world. Hsu dedicates in researching “ Game Play" of mutual interaction with the audience, production included “Mario Game Time” and “Baby in Cart."Hsu has also served as the art consultant for the Tainan Street Art Competival. Currently, in his capacity as director of the Taiwan Street Arts and Culture Development Association (Str. ACD), he strives to create an environment conducive to promoting street art in Taiwan whilst also making connections with foreign art festivals and performers with the hope of creating a thriving street art scene.
