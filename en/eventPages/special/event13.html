13ART TOUR

For three consecutive weekends, there will be themed experiential tours focusing on coffee, tea mountains, and plantations. These tours will offer limited spots and are open for registration. Individuals who are interested are welcome to join and embrace a lifestyle enriched with aesthetic experiences.

Brew a cup of healing time.

A pour over coffee expert will share the basic principles of pour over brewing and coffee tasting, as well as provide knowledge about the health benefits of coffee. In addition to a DIY coffee cup activity, a guided tour of the ceramic art park will also be provided.
▧ Meeting point: The service counter in the lobby on the 1st floor of the Ceramic Museum.
▧ Registration time: Registration opens at 2:00 PM on June 21 (Wednesday).
▧ Fee: NTD 450 per person.
▧ Note: Please join the Ceramic Academy as a member before registering.
