Oh my, a mysterious black hole has appeared in the sky! It's turning the vibrant and colorful world into an unfathomable abyss! The performance is based on interactive tours, where the performers take on the role of Captain Sugar, the leader of the Cosmic Guardians, and recruit audience members as team members. Together, they embark on a journey into the unknown universe to discover the cause of the rift and attempt to save the Earth, a world filled with diverse cultures.
