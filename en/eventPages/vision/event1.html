01SUNLIGHT BOULEVARD
MRT SKYWALK
LIGHTING INSTALLATION
BLACK IRON / NEON TUBES / PVC PRINT
DEPENDS ON THE VENUE

Located at the main entrance of the park, this iconic artwork uses light and colors to reveal the festive imagery and herald the flourishing of the new territory. Rooted in local collaboration, it combines traditional craftsmanship such as neon lights and metalwork to visually transform the sunrise and the ever-changing dust particles in the air. The alternating sun and moon at the end of the passage symbolize the eternal axis of faith within the cycles of time.

LIU CHIH–HUNG

Born in Hsinchu in 1985, this artist’s recent works focus on capturing contemporary life experiences through a daily perspective. Their artistic practice encompasses various mediums including painting, installation, landscape, video, found objects, and print publications, exploring the intersections of “materiality and agency,” “transformation of linguistic forms,” and “local relational connections.”
