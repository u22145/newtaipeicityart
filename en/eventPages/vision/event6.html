LARGE-SCALE KITE PROJECT
MIXED MEDIA
DEPENDS ON THE VENUE

The large-scale kite project, conducted for three consecutive weeks, is a live performance in the sky. It intertwines both new creations and past works, creating an instant focal point that captures everyone’s gaze. As imagination soars, it becomes a collective invocation, offering blessings to the newly emerged land. The unexpected winds that sway traditional craftsmanship become a spontaneous declaration of evolution within this improvised context.


※A large-scale kite project displayed on occasion. The performance takes place on 7/2, 7/9, 7/16 from 3 pm to 4 pm, and the works on display are subject to the weather conditions and wind direction at that time.

BUTEO HUANG

Born in Sanxia, New Taipei City in 1962, the artist has had a creative career spanning over forty years. Through architectural techniques, the artist has deconstructed kites and integrated them into a synthesis of science, craftsmanship, and fine art. The artworks not only showcase aesthetic creativity and exquisite craftsmanship but also resemble a miniature sculpture that can fly. This redefines the historical perception of kites as traditional children’s toys, elevating them to the realm of installation art and performance art.
