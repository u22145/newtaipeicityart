The epic of the universe and all things reflects the unique characteristics of the environment and gives birth to highly distinctive regional cultures. The performance is based on the Yingge Mythology, with the story of Nüwa mending the sky with mysterious stones, sculpting human forms from clay, and defeating the monstrous beast Ao and demons with the help of her army. It leads the audience into a fantastical realm, immersing them in the primal power of the land.

ACTORSHIP

Founded in 2020, Actorship is dedicated to the core concept of “courses as creations.” They take the training courses of performers as a starting point for collective creativity and focus on the concept of “life experience” to cultivate internal self-development and performance skills. Recent works include “Auntie & Uncle Shakespeare in Ximen,” “Nuit Blanche,” and “The Drag Queen.
