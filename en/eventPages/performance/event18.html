STAY WITH US! This powerful call to action signifies the energetic form of parkour, where the body adapts to the terrain and freely traverses between architectural landscapes. It harnesses the newfound energy and moves in harmony with nature and all living things, creating a constantly changing and evolving movement.

FUN ACTION

Established in 2014, Fun Action is a professional parkour team in Taiwan, dedicated to helping talented athletes grow together and showcasing the beauty and value of sports. Their performances have been showcased in events such as the Suisui Culture Market festival at the Songshan Cultural and Creative Park and the Nuit Blanche Flash Mob, as well as occasional flash competitions in major cities across Taiwan.
