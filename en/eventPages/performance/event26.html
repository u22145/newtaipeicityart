26HAPPINESS IN YUAN

PARADE PERFORMANCE
FROM MRT SKYWALK（NEAR THE SIDE OF NEW NEW TAIPEI CITY ART MUSEUM PARK）TO WEST LAWN
7/8 SAT. 15:00-15:30、17:00-17:30
7/9 SUN. 15:00-15:30、17:00-17:30

A kind of familiar yet new connection is quietly taking place in the New Art Land. It is the starting point of communication and a journey of inclusiveness. Inspired by the tradition of seeking divination at Taiwanese temples, the performance uses body language to interpret fortune-telling poetry. The aim is to interact with the audience to create a dance that provides “relief from worries,” and thus experience divination through the vocabulary of dance.

LES PETITES CHOSES PRODUCTION

Established in 2014, Les Petites Choses Production engages in participatory art practices, continuously reimagining and creating value in the field of contemporary arts and cultural experiential education in non-traditional exhibition spaces. Together with civil society, they strive to build a more sustainable art support network. Their performances have been showcased at significant domestic and international arts festivals, including the Edinburgh Festival Fringe and the Taiwan International Festival of Arts (TIFA).
