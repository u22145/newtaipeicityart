The collective chanting of diverse ethnic groups tells stories of daily life and resonates with the cultural wisdom passed down through generations. Starting with indigenous language singing, the performance uses simple and melodious rhythms to recount the bits and pieces of indigenous people’s lives. It is a grounded and heartwarming reminiscence, symbolizing joy and unity that gather and blend together.

PALIULIUS(8664)

Established in 2003, Paliulius(8664) conveys the ideals of young indigenous people dedicating themselves to their land and culture through indigenous language composition and a fresh and timeless musical style. They are a rare indigenous-style vocal group among the new generation of indigenous musicians. Their performances have been nominated for awards such as the Golden Indie Music Awards, Golden Melody Award for Best Album in an Aboriginal Language - Vocal Category, Golden Melody Award for Best Aboriginal Singer - Vocal Category, and Golden Melody Award for Album of the Year.
