The streets of New Taipei City brim with local energy, radiating a diverse and innovative force. The performance draws inspiration from various street art forms and expands possibilities through interactive connections with the audience and the performance space. It aims to create a vibrant and diverse street culture experience.

STREET ACT LAB

Established in 2017, the Street Act Lab (SAL) focuses on "Learning," "Discussions," and "Presentations" as its three core principles. The SAL collaborates with directors to develop and nurture over 30 emerging street artists and has produced more than 20 works for art festivals. The performances have been a part of major art festivals in Asia, serving as an important platform for promoting Taiwan's street art festivals and works.
