15CIRCUS PARK PROJECT：GAME'S LABORATORY

STAGE PERFORMANCE

7/15 SAT. 16:30-17:00
7/16 SUN. 16:30-17:00

Using circus props, the performance transforms the space into a mobile playground. Rolling, chasing, and playing are key activities of childhood in the park. Starting from circus arts, the performance showcases body skills and prop manipulation to change the original appearance and rules of the space. It challenges the possibilities of danger and imagination, allowing us to break the rules and play together in this creative park.

CIRCUS GATE

Founded in 2015, Circus Gate has been actively participating in society through experimental creations using circus elements. They aim to bring the term “circus” beyond entertainment and establish it as a well-known cultural and spiritual symbol through interaction with the public. Their performances have been featured in various arts festivals, including the Taipei Children’s Arts Festival, Tainan Street Arts Festival, and Pingzhen Art Festival in Taoyuan.
