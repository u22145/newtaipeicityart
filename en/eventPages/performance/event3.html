Under the warm glow of the lingering twilight, an inclusive atmosphere permeates through the hustle and bustle, as people come together and swing into a scene filled with anticipation for the future. The swing dance, which has been passed down since the 1920s, not only allows the public to experience the joy of jazz music and swing dancing, but its inherent social nature also encourages interaction among participants. Through the energy of playfulness and free-spirited movement, the enjoyment of dancing together is deepened.

SwingTaiwan

Founded in 2012, SwingTaiwan brings people together through dance, specifically the swing dance that has been passed down for a century since the 1920s. It serves as a medium for embracing a new way of life and enriching social interactions during leisure time among a group of friends who love life. Their performances have been featured in events such as the Nuit Blanche Flash Mob, Summer Party at National Theater & National Concert Hall, and Vogue Style Picnic Day.
