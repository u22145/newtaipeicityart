The musical threads of longitude and latitude intertwine with the land, forming a migratory pulse of social and cultural contexts. By listening carefully, one can uncover a multitude of auditory textures. Aoi performs in a diverse range of languages, accompanied by serene and captivating melodies. They lead the audience to collectively weave songs, immersing themselves in a grounded and harmonious atmosphere.

aoi

Formed in 2017, Aoi blends together elements of folk, electronic, and indie music. Through their diverse language compositions and straightforward lyrics, coupled with emotionally resonant melodies, they crawl into the ears of their fans like insects and make themselves at home. Their debut album "ū lí ê kòo-sū" was nominated for the Best New Artist, Best Taiwanese Album and the Album of the Year Award at the 32nd Golden Melody Awards.
