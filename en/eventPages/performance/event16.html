The lively notes guide the dancers’ steps, and the combination and transformation of atmospheres are as diverse and blended as the New Art Land. With flamenco as the main theme, the performance uses joyful tunes, Farruca melodies, and festive rhythms to narrate various states of life. The dancers’ lively and playful movements resonate with the excitement of the festival’s opening.

GENIO DANCE GROUP

Established in 2000, Genio Dance Group combines ballet, contemporary dance, and indigenous music to create their unique style of flamenco that represents Taiwan. They have been dedicated to non-mainstream creative works on stage. Artistic Director Cufa has been actively involved in art care and educational programs, bringing their performances to various corners of life and providing warmth and care through dance.
