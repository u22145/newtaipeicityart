Migration, settlement, tracing back—each cycle of life’s politics weaves together to form the rich soil of culture. 汝妮DUNGI SAPOR combines Techno electronic music elements with indigenous singers and drummers in experimental performances, harnessing the energy of cross-domain elements to explore new possibilities for indigenous music and culture.

DUNGI SAPOR

Since university until now, 汝妮DUNGI SAPOR has continuously utilized Techno music to combine tribal, folk, and mystical elements, creating intense and powerful electronic music that is deeply connected to their cultural roots. Their 2021 release, “I HO YAN,” was designated as halftime music by the NBA G League and has recently made appearances at major music festivals in Taiwan, performing alongside European Techno DJ Monoloc.
