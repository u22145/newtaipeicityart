Though the pages may turn yellow, memories remain vivid and untouched. Classic works are revitalized in the present not out of nostalgia, but to showcase the continuous evolution and innovation. The performance inherits the timeless techniques of acrobatics, embracing diversity and transformation, and ushering in a new era of innovation in the art of acrobatics.

New Image Theater Group

Established in 1990, New Image Theater Group is rooted in acrobatics and incorporates elements from modern circus, children’s theater, and creative theater to enter the realm of contemporary theater. The troupe has been selected as an outstanding performing arts group by New Taipei City and has participated in numerous international events. They are also dedicated to nurturing the potential of underprivileged individuals and have received recognition with the Social and Arts Education Contribution Award.
