WATSON WU／SLACKLINEFORMOSA

SlacklineFormosa is a professional slackline team in Taiwan, established in 2018. The artist, Watson Wu, has participated in numerous highline slackline projects both domestically and internationally. Through the unique nature of walking on flat webbing, they have developed innovative performance styles that combine their observations and experiences. Their goal is to inspire the general public to live in the present moment, explore various lifestyles, challenge themselves, and enjoy the happiness that comes from being fully focused. They also aim to inject new energy into outdoor exploration education and the integration of arts and leisure activities in Taiwan.
