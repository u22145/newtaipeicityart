A harmonious sound announces "new voices" as a declaration of "rebirth," igniting the energy of the land, uplifting the newly born places, inspiring New Taipei, and motivating Taiwan. The performance piece adapts songs in different languages such as Chinese, Taiwanese, Hakka, Aboriginal, and English. In the form of a crossover suite, it leads the audience to experience the converging dynamics of diverse cultures, using the purest human voice, which is closest to the heart, to deliver the purest emotions.

Semiscon Vocal Band

Established in 2002, Semiscon Vocal Band specializes in performances using pure vocal sounds, breaking through traditional concepts and limitations of choral music. They emphasize providing the audience with a unique and immersive experience. Their performances have received numerous awards both domestically and internationally, and they have been invited to perform at prestigious events such as the Golden Horse Awards, Golden Bell Awards, National Day celebrations, and tours in European and American cities.
