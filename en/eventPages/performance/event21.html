Lines transform into imaginative forms, emanating the diverse colors and rhythms of the land. Ancient and modern meet in the journey of play, sparking a dialogue between tradition and contemporary. The performance moves through the surreal realm of amusement, integrating traditional circus elements. Within the train where the past and present converge, a fusion of harmony invites the audience to join in a romantic and heartwarming reverie.

THUNAR CIRCUS

Established in 2016, Thunar Circus sought to connect with and engage the public through their creative actions and perspectives, combining folk arts with contemporary circus practices in their exploration and experimentation. Their works aim to evoke genuine and poetic sensations and stimulate philosophical reflections on society. Some of their notable creations in recent years include “Main Agile,” “Melancholic Mambo,” and “Hung Tung’s Fantasy.”
