The cultural undercurrent lies dormant on the streets of New Taipei City, generating disruption through its free extension and transformation. A gathering force is about to erupt! New Taipei Street Dance Tide combines improvised creations within the urban landscape, featuring street dance freestyle battles and choreographed dance competitions. It leads the audience to witness the dynamic movement of diverse cultures converging on the New Art Land.

SEXY DANCER FAMILY

The team is composed of Pihsin Chiu, the founder of the Sexy Dancer brand. Pihsin has been involved in the dance industry for 16 years. Through the establishment of an exclusive clothing brand, organizing dozens of hip-hop parties, and three small-scale street dance competitions, they aim to promote street dance culture. Their performances take place sporadically in various urban spaces, and recently, they served as joint judges for the "First URBN Street Dance Art Festival" in France, promoting Taiwanese street dance culture to the world.
